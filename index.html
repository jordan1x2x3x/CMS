Process

Your Path to Convocation,
Simplified into Steps.

A clear, structured process from registration to your clearance certificate. No confusion, no long queues.

1

Create Your Account

Register with your MTU institutional email. Students use their matric number; officers use their department access code.

2

Submit Required Documents

Upload department-specific clearance forms, receipts, and certificates through the secure document portal.

3

Track Your Progress

Watch your clearance status update in real-time as each of the 15 departments reviews and responds to your request.

4

Download Your Certificate

Once all departments grant clearance, your official certificate is instantly available for download.
