Frequently Asked Questions
How do I submit a clearance request? expand_more
Go to your Dashboard and click the Start Clearance Request button. Select your clearance type (Graduation or Withdrawal), read and accept the declaration, then submit. Your request will be distributed to all 15 departments automatically.
Why is my Chapel clearance flagged? expand_more
A flagged status from Chapel usually means outstanding chapel attendance or tithe records. Visit the Clearance Status page, click on Chapel in the stepper, and follow the listed action steps — you may need to visit the Chapel Office in person or upload an attendance record.
What documents do I need to upload? expand_more
Required documents vary by department. The Document Uploads page lists each document with its current status. Only documents marked Action Needed or Not Started require your attention. Accepted formats: PDF, JPEG, PNG (max 5MB each).
Why can't I download my certificate yet? expand_more
Your final clearance certificate is only available after all 15 departments have approved your clearance. You currently have 1 flagged and several pending departments. Resolve the flagged Chapel issue first — visit Clearance Status for details.
How long does department approval take? expand_more
Processing times vary by department. Most departments process within 3–5 working days after submission. Departments like Library and Bursary may take longer if there are outstanding fees. If a department has not acted after 7 working days, contact the Registrar's Office.
I submitted a wrong document. Can I re-upload? expand_more
Yes. Go to Document Uploads, find the relevant document card, and upload a corrected file. If the document was already approved, you will need to contact the relevant department officer to re-open the submission window.
What is the difference between Graduation and Withdrawal clearance? expand_more
Graduation Clearance is for students who have completed their programme and are proceeding to receive their degree. Withdrawal Clearance is for students who are leaving the university before completing their programme. Both types require sign-off from all 15 departments.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? expand_more
On the Login page, click Forgot password? and enter your institution email address. A reset link will be sent to your personal recovery email (if set) or your MTU email. You can also update your password directly from Settings → Security.
My matric number or academic record is incorrect. Who do I contact? expand_more
Academic records (matric number, college, department, programme) are managed by the Registrar's Office and cannot be changed within the portal. Visit the Registrar's Office in person with your original admission letter and a valid student ID for corrections.
Can I cancel or withdraw my clearance application? expand_more
Yes. Go to Settings → Account and click Withdraw Application. This will cancel your active clearance request. Note: departments that have already approved your request will need to re-approve if you re-apply.
